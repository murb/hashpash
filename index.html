HashPash

Make unique passwords without writing them down. How? Read how it works.


How it works

  1. Enter the sitename (the hash) you want to create an unique password for
  2. Enter your (reusable) Pashword
  3. Copy the generated password (the mash) and you're save to go.

Save to go?

HashPash uses the strong scrypt function,which makes it practically impossible for someone who has obtained the generated mash to decrypt it back to yourvery private Pashword.

Don't trust this server?

Save this page somewhere you'd like to keep it save. It works even from your Documents-folder and/or without an internet connection!
